About Me

I am a Master in Management candidate at the University of Chicago Booth School of Business, building on my social science background from UChicago. As a research assistant at Booth, I contribute to behavioral science projects and am passionate about applying these insights to business strategy—particularly in the sports industry. Beyond academics, I’m proud to be a member of QuestBridge and the Hispanic Scholarship Fund communities. As QuestBridge chapter president, I led initiatives supporting underrepresented students, and through Illinois’ Reimagine Public Safety Act, I designed STEM curricula for at-risk youth. I aim to pivot into the sports industry after my program and look forward to connecting with others who share my interests in behavioral science, sports, and social impact!
